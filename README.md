# Swiggy-Instamart-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing Instamart grocery sales to generate business insights. Evaluates sales by product category, outlet location, and size, along with customer ratings. Includes KPIs like Total Sales, Avg Sales, Items, and Rating. Demonstrates data modeling, DAX, and interactive visualization skills.

The dashboard was designed to provide a clear overview of business performance using interactive visuals and filters, enabling users to drill down into specific categories and locations. Customer satisfaction was analyzed using rating metrics to understand product quality perception and its potential impact on sales. The project demonstrates the ability to clean and transform data, build data models, create DAX measures, and design user-friendly dashboards that communicate insights effectively to stakeholders.

Key KPIs included in the dashboard:
Total Sales – Represents the overall revenue generated from product sales
Average Sales – Indicates the average revenue contribution per product or transaction
Total Number of Items – Shows the total count of products sold or available in the dataset
Average Customer Rating – Reflects overall customer satisfaction and product quality perception

This project highlights strong analytical and visualization skills and showcases the ability to convert business problems into data-driven solutions. Future improvements can include profit margin analysis, time-based trend analysis, forecasting techniques, and implementation of advanced DAX calculations to enhance the depth of insights and business value.
